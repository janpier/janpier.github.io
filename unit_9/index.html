WELCOME

Since their appearance in late 2014, the visual art and music project known as Loosid has consistently defied easy categorization and constantly transcended labels so frequently found in the dance music scene. With an ethereal sound that first exploded onto the scene with a remix of John Mayers cover of Free Fallin, Loosid has been not-so-quiety gathering recognition with every release. After signing with heavyweight CAA, Loosid surpassed the 2 million view mark for Clouds (feat Raycee Jones) on Monstercat and delivered a massive remix of The Chainsmokers Roses(with over 1 Million Streams on Spotify). This has only served to increase the demand for their musical talents, as well as eagerly received remixes for Galantis, Gallant, Dropout, Don Diablo, and Matoma as loosid has successfully exported its surrealist sounds, flowing rhythms and warped percussions to eager audiences worldwide.
